What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is the study of matter, its interaction and phenomenon. Chemistry is the central science as it is applied to most fields like in medicine, biology, anatomy and physiology, electrical engineering, and many more that deals with matter, which is almost basically everything.

Because of its application in our world, it is important to learn chemistry as it is a foundation and prerequisite for most fields. The hard truth here is that chemistry is hard however, this should actually make chemistry more exciting to learn and solve more than boring and stressful because you will be pushed outside your comfort zone and make you a better person than you are before.
