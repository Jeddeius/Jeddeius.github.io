Classification of Matter

Terms and definitions

Atom
An atom is the smallest particle of an element
Element
An element is the purest form of a matter, each element consist of different numbers of protons.
Molecule
Molecules are matter composed of two or more atoms, it usually have unique properties distinct from the atoms it's composed of.
Note that I said molecules are composed of more than one atoms, not elements, which brings us to:
Compound
Compounds are molecules comprised of more than one elements
